This is an inline equation \\(a^2 + b^2 = c^2\\), and the following is a display equation:

$$E = mc^2$$

Definition

In a right-angled triangle, the square of the length of the hypotenuse is equal to the sum of the squares of the lengths of the other two sides.

Theorem

For any right-angled triangle with sides of lengths \(a\), \(b\), and \(c\), where \(c\) is the length of the hypotenuse, \(a^2 + b^2 = c^2\).
